Songwriter and Guitarist.

I write and perform my own music as a solo artist, but have played in various bands over the years. My music is heavily influenced by legendary blues guitarists, I'm a guitar nerd at heart and love to hear the instrument played in its purest form. As a one-man-band however, I like to add an electronic twist to give a fresh and original edge to my music, the main goal being listenability!

As well as performing my own music, I am also available for sessions and contracts, for example, I can produce backing tracks for singers, or provide guitar services for recording or performances.
